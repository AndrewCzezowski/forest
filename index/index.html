      Aspiring physics student. I recieved my BSc Honours Physics-Chemistry from the University of Manitoba in 2024. I've worked 2 km underground with the PICO Collaboration at SNOLAB. My research interests are in particle theory, currently focusing in dark matter candidates and in fundamental interactions.

      This site is a collection of my scientific notes created using Forester.

      The general principle is that I keep my notes as atomized concepts (up to the point that I deem is beneficial). I use links between notes to allow an emergent structure rather than imposing a hierchy on the ideas. I later transclude my notes into notebook overviews of the topic in order to conveniently see what I've covered.

      To navigate my forest press Ctrl+K and search, or use links between notes. Some notebooks I've made are available here.
